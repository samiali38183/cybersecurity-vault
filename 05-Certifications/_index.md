## How to use this folder
For each cert, the folder should contain:
- `Study-Plan.md` — chapter-by-chapter plan with target dates
- `Domain-Notes.md` (one per exam domain) — your personal notes
- `Practice-Exam-Log.md` — score history with weak areas
- `Post-Exam-Reflection.md` — written after passing (or failing) — what worked, what to do differently
